Delete Your Account

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Festival App account and all data associated with it.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open The Festival App Launch The Festival App on your iOS or Android device and make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Go to Settings Tap on your Profile tab, then tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top-right corner of the screen.
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone Scroll down to the bottom of the Settings screen until you see the "Danger Zone" section.
  4. Tap "Delete Account" Tap the "Delete Account" button. You will be asked to confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.
  5. Confirm Deletion Confirm your choice in the dialog. Your account, profile, and all associated data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

⚠ Before You Delete

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.
  • You will be signed out immediately and will no longer be able to log in with the same credentials.
  • Any content you have posted (posts, comments, chat messages) may be removed or anonymized.
  • Active subscriptions (if any) are not automatically cancelled. Please cancel any subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play before deleting your account.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, profile photo, bio)
  • Your festival interest categories and followed festivals
  • Your posts, comments, and reactions
  • Your chat messages and chat room memberships
  • Your location sharing data
  • Your festival attendance records and uploaded photos
  • Your job listings
  • Your leaderboard score and badges
  • Your push notification token and notification preferences
  • Your Firebase authentication credentials
